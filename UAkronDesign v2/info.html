About The Program

A B.F.A. in Graphic Design paves the way for a variety of careers as a visual communicator, including graphic design, web design, illustration or advanced ranks of art director or creative director.

The graphic design program in the Mary Schiller Myers School of Art encompasses the many specialized areas within the visual communications field. Our seven full-time faculty membersand industry-leading adjunct faculty provide diverse academic and professional expertise, while well-equipped graphic design studios enable students to gain experience with technology that is standard in the industry. The curriculum prepares students for this competitive field by emphasizing critical thinking and logical problem solving. View the B.F.A. Graphic Design Curriculum.

Advanced students have the opportunity to work with clients, printers, service bureaus and other professionals in the field. The students hone their design skills, business savvy, technical knowledge and teamwork abilities through a rigorous and diverse curriculum.
