"CREATE SOMETHING PEOPLE WANT TO SHARE"

Ashley Hauser

HONORS PROJECT

Creativity. Where can you find it? When does it hide? These questions have no definite answers, just opinions and experiences from different people in various professional fields. Creativity is an important aspect as to what makes you human. It allows for open thought and infinite ideas that have yet to be discovered. Your Inner Creativity explores this idea through quotes and excerpt from TED Talks.

For my honors project I wanted to explore more with video, specifically kinetic typography. Creating posters coupled by videos, I was able to take the viewer beyond a static poster and into a more interactive experience.
